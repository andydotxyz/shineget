Product summary

Product description / spec

Young or old, keen modeller or just a beginner, the Hornby railroad range answers the needs of many and is a perfect complement to the variety of locomotives, rolling stock, track, buildings, and accessories by Hornby.

  • 00 gauge.
  • DCC (digital) ready.
  • Space required 1090mm x 940mm.
  • Track layout: starter oval with Midi TrakMat.
  • 1 driver, 1 dummy driver plus 2 coach.
  • One supplied.
  • Size H8, W30, D80cm.
  • For ages 5 years and over.
  • EAN: 5010963411558.

WARNING(S):

  • Not suitable for children under 3 years old.
  • For domestic use only.
  • To be used under the direct supervision of an adult.

Very realistic Very nice model with good level of detail, the working head and tail lights really add to the realism of the product. 03 September 2013
lovely train the train itself is nicely finished and makes the characteristic clickety clack noise when it runs on the rails. It forward and rear lights (white and red) which change when you change direction. The carriages are very long and care must be taken to put them on the track. It is advisable to place the track on a solid surface or pinned to a board to prevent movement. If you running an inner track, this is important as the tolerance on the curves is tight as the carriages overhang the track. My only gripe is the faulty controller to which I shall be complaining to Hornby. I keep having to unplug and plug it in again as it seems to stop working for no apparent reason, Fortunately, I have another controller, 15 years old but much sturdier. I fear the Hornby quality is not what it used to be! 09 April 2013
